Zilzil Tibs Recipe

Description

Learn how to make an authentic Ethiopian dish by using our Zilzil Tibs Recipe. Here you will find all the ingredients you will need to make this traditional Ethiopian dish with all the necessary directions. Enjoy!

Ingredients

    Roasted Meat

  • 1 lb beef tenderloin (cut into 1/2-inch cubes)
  • 1 ¾ pounds chicken drumsticks, skin removed
  • ¼ cup grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 tablespoons niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter), divided
  • 1 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons berbere (Ethiopian spice blend)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons fine kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro leaves and stems

Steps

    Instructions Checklist

  1. Step 1
    Put the beef in a medium nonreactive bowl.
  2. Step 2
    Combine 3 tablespoons of the berbere, 2 tablespoons of the niter kibbeh, and the red wine in a small nonreactive bowl and mix into a paste.
  3. Step 3
    Scrape half of the paste over the beef and toss to coat. Set aside the remaining paste.
  4. Step 4
    Let the meat marinate at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  5. Step 5
    Heat 1 tablespoon of the niter kibbeh in a medium skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Step 6
    Add the beef and cook, tossing, until it just loses most of its red color, about 2 minutes.
  7. Step 7
    Transfer the beef to a plate using a slotted spoon.
  8. Step 8
    Reduce the heat to medium. Add the bell peppers and cook, stirring, until just beginning to wilt, about 5 minutes.
  9. Step 9
    Transfer the peppers with the slotted spoon to the plate with the beef.
  10. Step 10
    Add 1 tablespoon of the niter kibbeh and cook until heated.
  11. Step 11
    Add the onions and cook, stirring, until softened, about 10 minutes.
  12. Step 12
    Add the remaining spice paste, the chicken broth, and black pepper and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add salt to taste
  13. Step 13
    Return the beef and peppers and any accumulated juices to the pan with the onions, and cook to heat for about 2 minutes. Serve hot with injera.
